buddie! understands people. When buddie! set out to help people with their money, buddie! discovered that although people express three (3) money traits in varying degrees there is a dominant trait, like a default setting, that people resort to given the right circumstances.

buddie! has designed a personality test to help you discover your dominant trait and to provide you with the best financial advice to help you get better with money and eventually make your money work for you.

When you take the test and/or read through the buddie! profiles don't be surprised if you see yourself in all three descriptions, it is expected.

jollof master

Jollof Master, to you this life is for pampering and if no one pampers you, you will do it yourself.

You’ll most likely spend what is necessary to have fun, whether you are with your friends or by yourself...as long as it is enjoyment, you are down.

There is a dream life that you want to live, you may not know how to attain it but you just know you need to make more money to live it.

When the thought of an increase in income crosses your mind, you are excited because you are a step closer to living that life.

Most times, you buy what you like, when you want. Have you ever gotten to the end of the week or middle of the month only to ask, “omo, when/how did I spend this money?”

savvy saver

Savvy Saver, you are a save at all costs type of person.

No matter what happens, you want to save a little bit of the money that enters your account. For you, saving money provides a sense of security

Money management is all about saving for you, as long as you are saving, you are managing your money.

Your strategy to grow your wealth is to save as much as you can. You may feel safer with fixed investments because you don't want to lose your money.

Sometimes, you are a “save to spend person”. When you have saved your funds for a period of time, you spend on a few necessities/wants then you find yourself back at ground zero trying to build up your savings again.

money guy

Money Guy, you have a high awareness level of your money.

You know when it comes in and when it goes out, you go the extra mile to track your finances daily. You like to plan your finances for the month to avoid stories that touch.

When you’re out with friends or treating yourself, you stick to the enjoyment budget you set for the month. Any enjoyment that exceeds your budget is not enjoyment.

Your desire to invest today is because you think about the future and how comfortable you want to be. As far as investment opportunities are concerned, you do your due diligence before making decisions about where to put your money.

You are constantly looking for new ways to grow your wealth e.g. stock market, bitcoins, etc.
